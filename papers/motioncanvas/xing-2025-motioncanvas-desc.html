MotionCanvas: Cinematic Shot Design (SIGGRAPH 2025)
With the rapid pace of video generation by diffusion models, the time is now ripe to facilitate film director control. This includes particularly control over the camera, the movement of characters, and even detailed control over the pose of these characters. We developed a framework, called MotionCanvas, that facilitates these multiple levels of controls, and offers a way to translate such director's 3D control into 2D conditions that video diffusion models can better understand.
