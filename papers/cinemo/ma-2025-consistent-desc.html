Cinemo: Consistent and Controllable Image Animation (CVPR2025)
Instead of directly denoising the video frames, we denoise the motion residuals between frame 1 and other frames. This approach can better preserve the appearance across time and suppresses the abrupt motion changes. To enhance the control of dynamics, we design a more explicit way to specify the dynamics as a condition to the i2v video diffusion model.
