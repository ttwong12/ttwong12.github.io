AppareKappore © Kanno Hirosh 		MangaSeg: Comprehensive Segmentation Annotations for Manga109 (CVPR2025)
The domain gap between natural photos and manga images poses a challenge for existing neural network models. Moreover, current manga datasets lack annotations suitable for high-quality segmentation. To address this, the goal of this project is to provide high-quality manga segmentation annotation on Manga109 dataset, which allows models trained on our dataset to overcome screentone and black-and-white color distribution interference. We hope this can enable more exciting and interesting downstream manga applications.
