Screentone-Preserved Manga Retargeting (EG2025)
Manga retargeting facilitates the display of manga on display with various aspect ratios and resolutions, without redrawing the manga all over again. While existing retargeting methods can handle natural photographs, they can hardly handle manga well. While the outline structure of manga can be easily resized, the screentones have to be preserved in both aspect ratio and resolution. The major challenge lies on the generation of aligned screentones. In this work, we shall focus on this precise alignment of screentones during the generation.
